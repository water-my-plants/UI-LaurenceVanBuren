Water My Plants

Track your plants' watering schedule automatically, without the guessing game.

No Plant Left Behind!

Watering plants is easy. Remembering when and how much water to give each one? That's another story. And if you've got more than a few leafy friends in your house, things can get out of order fast. With the Water My Plants app, you can ditch the memory game and watch your plants flourish!

Track Each Plant

Add a name, location and watering cycle for each plant you want to monitor. If a plant has special needs, enter notes into the description field.

Edit Your List

Managing and making edits to your list is simple. Quickly add new plants, edit plant info (e.g. watering cycle, location) or easily remove a plant from your list if necessary.

Prompt Alerts

Create and set your own alerts when it's time to water your plants and get notifications automatically sent to your mobile device.

Sit Back

When your plants are happy, you will be too! Keeping a steady watering schedule will help your plants live long, healthy lives!
